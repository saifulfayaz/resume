About Me

A clean overview of my academic journey, technical direction and practical industry exposure.

I am a final-year Electrical Engineering student at the University of Kashmir, on track to graduate in 2026. My academic journey is driven by a strong interest in electrical systems, power infrastructure and the engineering principles behind modern energy networks.

Alongside my engineering programme, I hold an NCVT-certified COPA diploma from ITI Bandipora, giving me a practical computing and IT foundation that complements my core technical background.

My internship at the Kishanganga Hydroelectric Power Station under NHPC Limited gave me first-hand exposure to utility-scale power generation, high-voltage substations, grid-level operations and safety practices in a live power environment.

I come from Bandipora, a district with strong renewable energy potential, and I am motivated to contribute to reliable and sustainable energy development.
