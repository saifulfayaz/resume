About me

A working overview of my academic direction, credentials and time inside a live power utility.

I'm a B.Tech Electrical Engineering graduate, Class of 2026, from the University of Kashmir. My focus sits on electrical systems, power infrastructure and the engineering behind modern energy networks — not the theory in isolation, but how it holds up in an actual substation.

My internship at the Kishanganga Hydroelectric Power Station under NHPC Limited put me in front of utility-scale generation, HV substations and grid-level operations — and the safety discipline that runs underneath all of it.

I'm from Bandipora, a district with real renewable-energy potential, which is a good part of why reliable, sustainable energy infrastructure is the direction I want to build a career in.
